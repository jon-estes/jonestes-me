About me

Jon Estes

Chinese American — collector, coach, card player, dad of three.

I'm a Chinese American with a love for math, a passion for competition, and a collector's heart. Whether it's coins, Rubik's cubes, poker chips, shoes, or anything worth hunting down — if it's worth collecting, I'm probably already on it.


I spent years as a varsity soccer head coach for both boys and girls teams — there's nothing quite like watching a team click. I also took my love of math abroad, volunteering with the Peace Corps in Sierra Leone and leaving a piece of my heart in West Africa.


Off the field I'm a poker player and former card dealer who takes the game seriously, a dedicated shopper, and most importantly the proud father of three baby girls — Annie, Clem, and Estella.
