What is a Mommy Makeover?

A mommy makeover is a personalized combination of cosmetic surgery procedures designed to restore a woman's body after pregnancy, childbirth, and breastfeeding. There is no single set procedure — it's customized to each patient's goals. Most commonly includes a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty), breast surgery (lift, augmentation, or both), and liposuction. Additional procedures can include a Brazilian butt lift, labiaplasty, arm lift, or thigh lift. All procedures are typically performed in a single surgery session under general anesthesia, reducing overall recovery time and cost compared to doing each separately.

Smart planning

Tips for Ashley 💕

🏥
Always go board certified
Every surgeon on this list is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. Never settle for a "cosmetic surgeon" without this credential — it's the most important safety factor.
📋
Book 3 consultations minimum
Most consults are free or $100 (credited to surgery). Seeing 3 surgeons gives you a baseline for pricing, and helps you find the personality fit that matters during recovery.
⚖️
Be at stable weight first
All surgeons recommend waiting until you're at or near your goal weight and finished having children. Pregnancy or major weight change after surgery can undo results.
💳
Financing is normal & available
Nearly every practice on this list offers CareCredit, M-Lend Financial, or in-house financing. No shame in financing — most patients do. CareCredit often has 0% interest promotional periods.
📸
Study before/after galleries hard
Every surgeon's aesthetic sensibility is slightly different. Spend time in the before/after gallery before booking a consult — find surgeons whose natural results match the look Ashley wants.
🗓️
Plan 4–6 weeks off
Recovery from a full mommy makeover is 2–6 weeks depending on procedures. Plan for childcare, household help, and no lifting for 4+ weeks. Time it around family schedules carefully.
🚭
No smoking — strict rule
Every surgeon requires stopping smoking 4+ weeks before and after surgery. Nicotine dramatically impairs healing and can cause serious complications. This is non-negotiable.
💬
Read RealSelf reviews
RealSelf has verified patient reviews with before/after photos for most surgeons on this list. It's the most honest source for what the actual patient experience is like post-surgery.

Important note: This page is research-only and compiled from publicly available information as of May 2026. Pricing is estimated and varies significantly based on the specific procedures included. Always verify pricing, credentials, and availability directly with each provider.
