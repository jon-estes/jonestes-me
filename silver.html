Context
⚖️
Troy ounce
Silver is priced per troy ounce — 31.1 grams. Different from the avoirdupois ounce (28.35g) used in everyday measurements.
🌍
Spot vs. physical
Spot price is the current market rate for immediate delivery. Physical silver (coins, bars) usually costs more due to premiums and fabrication.
🏭
Industrial demand
Over 50% of silver demand is industrial — used in solar panels, electronics, and EV batteries — meaning it tracks the economy as much as it tracks fear.
📊
Gold/silver ratio
Historically, it takes 15–80 oz of silver to buy 1 oz of gold. The ratio is a common way to gauge relative value between the two metals.
