Why Poker?

Poker isn't just a card game — the skills it exercises are ones we use every day as a training team.

In poker — like in medical device work — you evaluate information quickly and decide under pressure.

🎯 Rules-Based Thinking

Hands follow a clear hierarchy. Mastering rules and exceptions is what every trainer does daily.

🤝 Team Bonding

Shared knowledge of a fun topic builds camaraderie across the team.
