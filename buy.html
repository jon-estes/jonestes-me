Know Your Marketplace30-second cheat sheet
AliExpress
  • Retail quantities, no MOQ — best for testing a product
  • Sort by orders to surface proven sellers
  • "Choice" listings = faster shipping, vetted sellers
  • 2–4 week shipping typical; buyer protection is decent
Alibaba
  • Wholesale/factory direct — MOQs of 50–500+ common
  • Listed prices are starting points; message for real quotes
  • Look for Verified Supplier + Trade Assurance
  • Best per-unit price once you know what sells
Temu
  • Cheapest sticker price, free shipping over threshold
  • Quality is a lottery — order a sample first
  • Weak for specialty items like .999 copper bullion
  • Good for stream supplies, packaging, accessories
Copper rounds specifically
  • Verify ".999 fine" and true 1 oz AVDP weight in specs
  • Copper spot is pennies/oz — you're paying for minting
  • Compare against US dealers (Golden State Mint, JM Bullion) at volume — shipping can erase overseas savings
  • Ask Alibaba sellers for assay/material certs on big orders
Listed prices on Alibaba are negotiable openers — always factor MOQ and freight before comparing. · jonestes.me
