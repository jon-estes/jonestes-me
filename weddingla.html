SoCal Coast Venue Options 🌊

The Orange County and Laguna Beach coastline is one of the most stunning in California — dramatic sea cliffs, golden coves, and crystal-clear Pacific water. Disneyland is 20–30 minutes from Laguna Beach, making this the perfect combo: beach wedding for Ashley, Disneyland trip for Annie, Clem, and Estella. It's 7–8 hours from Sacramento by car, 1.5 hrs by plane. Best of both worlds: coastal ceremony + theme park magic.

Montage Laguna Beach

⭐ Top Pick Laguna Beach Cliffside Luxury Ocean Views
9.8/10

The crown jewel of California coastal weddings. Montage Laguna Beach sits atop dramatic sea cliffs above a private cove, with 360° Pacific Ocean views. Consistently rated one of the top 5 wedding venues in California. Ceremonies happen on the oceanfront lawn or bluff overlooking the ocean — waves crashing below, sunset light painting everything gold. The resort is pure luxury: private beach access, cliff-side pools, spa, and world-class dining. 25 minutes from Disneyland. This is the venue where Ashley's coastal dream meets California luxury at its finest.

Surf & Sand Resort

Ashley's Vibe Laguna Beach On the Sand Toes in the Sand
9.4/10

The Surf & Sand Resort in Laguna Beach puts you literally on the sand — not above a cliff, not near a beach, but directly on it. This is the most "Ashley" venue on this list: toes in the sand, waves within reach, ocean backdrop for the ceremony. The resort sits on a private stretch of Laguna Beach with ceremonies held right on the beach. The restaurant (Splashes) has floor-to-ceiling ocean views. 25 minutes from Disneyland. Slightly more affordable than Montage but equally stunning for a beach-forward couple.

Pelican Hill Resort

Newport Coast Luxury Estate Ocean Views
9.2/10

A palatial Italian Renaissance-style resort on Newport Coast with sweeping views of the Pacific and Catalina Island. Pelican Hill is grand, dramatic, and architectural — ceremony in the Coliseum Bowl (a literal open-air coliseum with ocean views) or the Garden Terrace. The resort has a legendary circular pool, two championship golf courses, and is 20 minutes from Disneyland and 10 minutes from Newport Beach. Perfect for couples who want something that feels more estate-grand than beach-casual.

Laguna Cliffs Marriott

Best Value Dana Point Coastal Views Family Friendly
8.6/10

Perched above the Pacific in Dana Point, the Laguna Cliffs Marriott offers breathtaking coastal views without the Montage price tag. The resort has a beautiful outdoor terrace for ceremonies with sweeping ocean panoramas, a harbor view, and easy access to the beach below. Dana Point is a charming surf town known as the "Dolphin & Whale Watching Capital of the World." It's 30 minutes from Disneyland, right on the coast, and more affordable than Laguna Beach's luxury properties. Great for larger guest lists.

Planning Tips for SoCal Coast July 2027 🗓️
🎡
Disneyland is 20–30 min away
The magic of this option: beach wedding for Ashley, then Disneyland for Annie, Clem, and Estella the next day. It's the only venue location that does both. Jon wins too — Downtown Disney has poker lounges nearby.
🌊
Ashley's beach requirement: met
Laguna Beach and Dana Point are among the most beautiful stretches of California coast. Surf & Sand puts the ceremony literally on the sand. Montage has private beach access. Ashley will love it.
☀️
Perfect July weather
Orange County in July is consistently 72–78°F with ocean breezes and minimal rain. Mornings can be overcast ("June Gloom") but afternoons clear — perfect for a 5pm ceremony.
🚗
Easy for family
7–8 hours by car from Sacramento, 1.5 hrs by plane. Your mom, dad, and sister can make the trip with minimal hassle. John Wayne Airport (SNA) is 20 min away.
📅
Book by early 2026
Laguna Beach is one of the most in-demand wedding destinations in Southern California. July weekends go fast. Contact venues by January 2026 at the latest for July 20, 2027.
🛍️
Ashley will be in shopping heaven
Laguna Beach's art galleries and boutiques, Fashion Island in Newport Beach, and South Coast Plaza (one of the most upscale malls in America) are all within 20 minutes. Pre-wedding shopping trip confirmed.
